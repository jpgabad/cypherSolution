Disaster Recovery

If your business falls prey to an attack that leads to downtime, it's crucial that you have a plan ready to let you recover quickly. Even something as simple as a server crash could easily result in losing data, revenue, and productivity if you don't have a plan in place. When we begin working with a client, we take time to assess their infrastructure and how it works – including the mission-critical systems, which take priority in the recovery process. Learning about your architecture, we can understand the best path for protecting your data.

Cypher can be instrumental in helping you put together a Disaster Recovery Plan – these are critical for any company. These plans work as a guide for resuming your operation after an incident, such as a DDoS attack. Even with the strongest cyber-security at your disposal, it's still vital that you make the proper preparations just in case. No two recovery plans are the same between businesses, so your staff cannot just follow online templates; they need professional help, and that is where we come in.

With our disaster recovery services, you can quickly bounce back from any downtime or setback.

Expert Security Consultancy

Though every network is unique, we're aware of specific trends and commonalities, which still allow us to provide general recommendations about the state of your network. We all contend with cyber-security threats, and our advice can help protect you against them, letting your staff keep working and your customers keep browsing. Our passionate experts take security seriously, and you're always welcome to discuss your security needs with us – we'll only give you accurate and honest advice in return, and these recommendations could save the whole company from downtime.

On top of this, we can educate your staff on the many security threats circulating online, teaching them how to spot phishing scams, recognise spoofed emails that might seem to be from within the company and other security-related threats. Many security problems begin with human error – and the most significant step to preventing this is teaching staff how to react to anything that seems amiss. Insight is the best defence against cyber-attacks, and knowing the many forms these might take is paramount.

By choosing us to consult on your online security, you and your staff can learn everything there is to know about protecting your network.

Network Security And Monitoring

Companies can run into general network issues – especially if traffic is higher than anticipated. Having an expert team on hand to assess your system and diagnose any current or potential problem might be just what you need, as this could expose instabilities in your network that may slow it down. This is another core pillar of our service; we use our equipment to keep track of your network's overall performance and provide recommendations on how you can improve this. This data doesn't just help us respond to threats but also shows how you can speed up your system.

Using penetration testing practices, we can simulate how a hacker would attack your website and assess how easy or difficult it would be for them. This lets you detect security issues long before anyone else can and gives you a chance to correct them and fortify your system until it's impenetrable. We test the network's defences and uncover what would be at threat if they gained access. We also run these tests as if we know nothing about your network; this 'black box testing' gives a more accurate simulation.

By letting Cypher monitor your network, we can ensure you understand how well-equipped it is to handle a range of security risks.
